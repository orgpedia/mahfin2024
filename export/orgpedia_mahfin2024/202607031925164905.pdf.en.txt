The Supplementary Statement of Expenditure for the year 2026-2027 was submitted to the Legislature on 22nd June, 2026. This supplementary expenditure has now been approved by the Assembly.
Supplement to be included in the appropriations for the financial year ending March 31, 2027
The Appropriation Bill. The Governor has assented and the same has been published as the Maharashtra (Second Supplement) Appropriation Act, 2026 (Maharashtra Act No. 24 of 2026) in the Government of Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary, Part IV, dated 03rd July, 2026.
2. Survey Ministries are being informed that proposals for distribution of funds for Supplementary Demand approved by them under the Supplementary Appropriation Act should be submitted directly to the Finance Department on the basis of the original demand for Supplementary Demand. The entire proposal should not be sent to the Finance Department unless there are certain reasons for the delivery of the supplementary demand if the original provision is suitable.
However, under these Government Circulars, the Central share of State share of Centrally Sponsored Schemes sanctioned through Supplementary Demands in the June-July, 2026 session is being distributed over the BEAMS system within a ceiling of 100%. The administrative departments shall strictly follow the guidelines contained in the Government Circular No. 2 under reference while disbursing the said funds at their level.
The Government Circular is available on the Government of Maharashtra website www.maharashtra.gov.in.
